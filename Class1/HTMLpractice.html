Let's remember to use semantic, meaningful elements instead of presentation elements

To truly understand why using semantic elements is important, we need to think beyond our own computers. Our audience should not simply be able-bodied, visual users. Users that are visually impaired will use screen readers that read the contents of the webpage to them aurally.

An underlined piece of text is of no interest to a visually impaired user. If the underline is being used to emphasize content, the screen-reader will simply ignore it. In your markup, use an element as it has semantic meaning.
